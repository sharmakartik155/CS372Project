WHAT WE DO

At Pet Tracker, our goal is to ensure the saftey of your furry friend.
When you register with Pet Tracker, we will provide you with a QR code
that will be placed on your pets collar. In the unfortunate event that your
pet runs away, a friendly stranger will be able to scan your pets collar
and recieve all the neccessary information needed to return your pet
safetly.
